1. Discovering how to relax your mind can be a daunting task, but it is an essential part of maintaining mental health and wellbeing.         
2. Start by taking some time out for yourself each day to focus on calming activities such as yoga, meditation, or simply listening to music. 
3. Make sure you get enough sleep each night and try to stick to a regular sleep schedule. 
4. Take breaks throughout the day and practice deep breathing exercises when feeling overwhelmed or stressed out. 
5. Lastly, find ways to reduce stress in your life such as avoiding negative people or situations, setting boundaries with others, and engaging in positive self-talk.
